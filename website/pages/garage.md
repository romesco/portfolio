Welcome to the garage. This is the workshop out back: half-built tools,
interactive sketches, and prototypes that aren't ready for the front of the
shop, and a few that never will be.

The guiding spirit here is Bret Victor's: an idea you can poke at beats one you
can only read about. So where it fits, the things in this room are meant to be
played with, not just looked at.

Nothing here is finished. That's the point. Things move around, so check back.
